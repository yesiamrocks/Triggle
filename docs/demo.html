Triggle Playground

The triggle.js library lets you easily add interactive animations to your HTML elements using data- attributes. Here's a rundown of each trigger type in your demo: event types.

Click

Click Me 
<div
  class="cssanimation"
  data-triggle="click"
  data-triggle-class="ca__fx-wiggle"
  data-triggle-reset="true">
  Click Me
</div>

data-triggle="click": This is one of the most common event types. The animation defined by data-triggle-class will fire when you click on the element.
